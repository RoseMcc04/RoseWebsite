Introduction

Hello, my name is Rose McCormack and I am an aspiring Computer Scientist. I hope that my website will allow people to look at my academic and personal resources I currently use to expand my knowledge on applications and theory within the field of Computer Science, along with my passion for learning most things.

I also hope that I can use my website as an expression of my interests. In August of 2007, I was diagnosed as PDD-NOS and ASD (Pervaisive Developmental Delay - Not Otherwise Specified, Autism Spectrum Disorder). This affects my ability to express myself creatively along with hampering my communication and social skills.

My Interests

I have many interests, however, most of them are within the field of computer science. This is due to a newfound love for learning after starting my transition, as well as my enjoyment in learning new problem-solving skills. My computer science interests are in the analysis of algorithms and learning about different frameworks for software design and development. On the contrary, I enjoy learning about geography and popular culture, specifically with geopolitics and the sociology of the modern internet.
